About Us

Our Story

Red Curry House was born from a passion for sharing authentic Thai flavors right here in Nuremberg. Our chefs use fresh, locally sourced ingredients and secret family recipes passed down through generations. With every dish, we invite you to discover the vibrant taste and warm spirit of Thailand. Enjoy unforgettable moments with us—where every guest is family!
